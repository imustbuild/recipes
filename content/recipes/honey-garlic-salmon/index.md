  - "Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat."
  - "Place salmon skin-side up in the skillet. Sear for 3-4 minutes until golden brown."
  - "Flip salmon skin-side down. Pour honey garlic mixture over the fillets."
  - "Transfer skillet to oven and broil for 5-7 minutes until salmon is cooked through and glaze is caramelized."

The broiler is the secret weapon here. It caramelizes the honey glaze into something magical while keeping the salmon perfectly moist.

## Tips for Perfect Salmon

- Let the salmon come to room temperature for 15 minutes before cooking
- Don't move the fish once it hits the pan - let it develop that crust
- The fish is done when it flakes easily with a fork
