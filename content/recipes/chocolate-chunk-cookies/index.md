These are **the** chocolate chip cookies. The brown butter adds an incredible depth of flavor that takes these from good to unforgettable.

## The Science

- **Brown butter** = Maillard reaction = nutty, caramel-like flavor
- **Chilling the dough** = solidifies the fat = cookies spread less = thicker result  
- **Underbaking slightly** = carryover cooking = gooey centers

The 1-hour chill time is non-negotiable. I know, I know. But trust me. The cookies will be significantly better.
